BeLive

This application is a big project that is still in the works but was invented with the hope that it will make life easier for those who are less fortunate to have the tools that we do today in our society. This application provides the medical attention for people who are in areas with no doctors or lack of trust in the medical system in their society. Therefore this application will act as a doctor or help guide the patient on a path to curing or temporarily easing pain in the area of need. This is done through a database storing medical information relevant to the geographical location where the person is and all know medical information. The program prompts for their name and other revelant information if they would like to provide it and then asks them to select the area on the body where the pain is and then provide an explanation of what the issue is and how they feel. This will result in the application searching through the database, which is stored locally in in the application, based on keywords in the description provided by the user. Then the application will list multiple treatments that the user can find and gain access of within their area and therefore treat the injury momentarily. An emergency feature is yet to be added along with a image capturing part that allow the user to take a photo of their injury and it will be sent to doctors to diagnose and attempt to solve.
